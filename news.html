California Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy applies to all clients and potential clients, including California residents.

Our Privacy Policy explains how Cash Recovery Now collects, uses, shares and protects the privacy of your personal information. It will apply to any personal information you provide to us when using our site or contacting us in person, by phone, fax, text messaging or e-mail.

What Information Do We Collect?

On our site we may collect details such as your name, company name, organization name, e-mail address, phone numbers and information relevant to your unclaimed money claim. We may collect additional personal information about you when we contact you directly in person, by phone, fax, text messaging or e-mail. On our site we will not collect any personal information about you without your consent.

How Do We Use Your Personal Information?

We collect your personal information for the following purposes:

1. To contact you about your unclaimed money.
2. To verify that you are the rightful claimant of the unclaimed money we have discovered.
3. To provide the information and required documents to the claim departments of government agencies after we receive your written approval to do so.

Disclosure of Personal Information to Third Parties

We may disclose your personal information to trusted third parties for the purpose of skip tracing, unclaimed money research, joint ventures with other asset recovery companies or related purposes.

Security of Your Personal Information

We will securely hold your personal information and we will NOT trade, rent or sell it. We will take all reasonable steps to keep secure any information which we hold about you. Personal information may be stored electronically on our computer system. Firewalls, passwords, anti-virus software and email filters act to protect all our electronic information. Our site employs Secure Sockets Layering (SSL) to encrypt all your personal information such as name, message, phone number and e-mail address before it travels over the Internet.

Unless we receive a written testimonial from you, we will NOT publicize or advertise that we recovered your unclaimed money.

Changes to This Privacy Statement

We may amend this Privacy Policy as our business requirements or the law changes. Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be updated on our site, so please visit this page periodically to ensure that you have our most current privacy policy.

Resolving Your Concerns

If you have any concerns on how your personal information is collected or used or you want to request the personal information Cash Recovery Now has collected about you, please feel free to contact us at (800) 579-5050 or at info@cashrecoverynow.com. We will respond to your query, request or complaint as soon as possible.
