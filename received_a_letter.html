About Our Company

Cash Recovery Now is a research and recovery company that audits numerous databases digging through their records in search of recoverable unclaimed, missing, hidden or abandoned assets. The money belongs to individuals, partnerships, large and small corporations, LLCs, estates, police departments, law firms, hospitals, physicians, trustees, government agencies, banks, title companies, insurance companies, churches, schools, private organizations and many more.

Our company's mission is to recover and return these assets before they are lost forever through a legal process called escheatment. We estimate that more than $100 billion is languishing in various unclaimed property funds nationwide and many billions of this money is escheated (confiscated) every year. Our research has determined that some of this money belongs to you!
